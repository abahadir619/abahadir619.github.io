    In LolCode, "BTW" followed by a phrase is called a comment, meaning it is not executed when the program is run. Another important thing to know is that the integer type is called NUMBR, and we can create two NUMBRs by assigning integer values to variables. In the code, there are two variables being declared and then added. Type in 2 and 4 for these variables respectively and then type the names of both variables like this: "VAR AN VAR2" on the third to last line!
    Here are the definitions of the mathematical operations in LolCode!
    SUM OF X AN Y X + Y (sum of X and Y)
    DIFF OF X AN Y X - Y (difference of X and Y)
    PRODUKT OF X AN Y X * Y (product of X and Y)
    QUOSHUNT OF X AN Y X / Y (X divided by Y)
    MOD OF X AN Y X modulo Y (X mod Y)
    BIGGR OF X AN Y X max Y (returns max)
    SMALLR OF X AN Y X min Y (returns min)
